# Daily Tech Briefing — 11 Jun 2026

### AI

**AI — The Rise of On-Device MoE Architectures for Edge Systems**
The shift from massive centralized LLMs to localized, hyper-efficient Mixture of Experts (MoE) models on edge devices has reached a tipping point. Standardized neural processing units (NPUs) now dynamically activate sub-networks, allowing edge gateways to run 30B-parameter class intelligence locally under a 5-watt envelope, bypassing cloud latency and privacy hurdles.

*Kink:* The primary bottleneck has shifted from raw compute capacity to the memory bandwidth required to hot-swap expert weights.

*Why it matters:*
* Reduces cloud inference costs by up to 70% for consumer applications.
